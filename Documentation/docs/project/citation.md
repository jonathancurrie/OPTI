If you have found a particular solver to be useful in your research then we encourage you to cite the solver. You can find the suggested citations under each solver on the [solvers](../solvers/index.md) page. However if you believe the OPTI Toolbox has been significant in your research as well, then use the citation below:

## OPTI Citation
J. Currie and D. I. Wilson, "OPTI: Lowering the Barrier Between Open Source Optimizers and the Industrial MATLAB User," *Foundations of Computer-Aided Process Operations,* Georgia, USA, 2012

See the paper [here](http://focapo.cheme.cmu.edu/2012/proceedings/data/papers/024.pdf).
